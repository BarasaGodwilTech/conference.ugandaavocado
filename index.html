      About the Conference

      The 1st Uganda Avocado Conference

      HASS Uganda (Hope Horticulture and Avocado Supply Services) in collaboration with other regional and international organizations in the Fruits and vegetables value chain in Uganda and beyond are pleased to announce to all stakeholders around the world that the 1st Uganda Avocado Conference 2026 will be held at the(uganda industrial research institute), Kampala, Uganda on 29th - 31st July 2026.

      Themed, "Advancing Uganda’s Avocado Sector for Sustainable Growth.", the event will draw participation of stakeholders from all over the world.

      Avocado Fruits & Vegetables Uganda 2026 is the largest conference of stakeholders in the world for Uganda. It is a platform specifically aimed at stimulating trade, technology, knowledge transfer and sharing.
