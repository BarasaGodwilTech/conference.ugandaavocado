      About the Congress

      The 1st International Africa Avocado Congress

      The AVOCADO SOCIETY OF Uganda in collaboration with other regional and international organizations in the Fruits and vegetables value chain in Africa and beyond are pleased to announce to all stakeholders around the world that the sixth International Africa Avocado Congress (2026 Avocado Fruits & Vegetables Africa) will be held at (location), Kampala, Uganda on 21st - 29th. May 2026.

      Themed, "Innovation, efficiency and sustainability", the event will draw participation of stakeholders from all over the world.

      Avocado Fruits & Vegetables Africa 2026 is the largest convergence of stakeholders in the world for Africa. It is a platform specifically aimed at stimulating trade, technology, knowledge transfer and sharing.
